How It Works

1. Submit for Free

Fill out one simple form with your bio, release details, and EPK. No signup or fees required.

2. We Pitch to Media

Our PR editors send your story to hundreds of active journalists, blogs, magazines, and playlists across genres.

3. Approval & Email Notification

Once approved, you’ll receive an email notification before your press materials are shared by one or more of our network of outlets.

Why BRiNSPOT Is Free

Every artist deserves access to real PR opportunities without paying thousands to agencies. BRiNSPOT is fully supported by donations and sponsors, allowing free submissions for all artists. We only grow when your story gets seen.

BRiNSPOT was created to help fellow artists get the exposure they deserve. You can support our mission by joining our Patreon — for less than $3/month, your release will be featured on our Top Artists page.
